Thanks for stopping by our table at this year's Student Activities Fair! If you didn't get a chance to sign up for our mailing list, email us at habitat@amherst.edu or join us at our Intro Meeting on Sunday the 20th!


Gingerbread Build

December 14, 2013




Thank you to everyone who helped with and participated in our annual Gingerbread Build event! We raised over $50.00 for Habitat International's Disaster Relief Fund and had a blast building cool houses and munching on delicious candy.

Congratulations to our winners!

Garfield Avenue Site

November 23, 2013




We got some excellent work done on the Garfield Avenue site today thanks to all of our dedicated volunteers! The entire first floor now has drywall installed on the walls and ceilings. Hooray!

Starting to look more like a home!

Super Typhoon Haiyan

November 18, 2013




Habitat International teams worked diligently to help in relief efforts for the devastating typhoon that struck the Philippines recently. We want to thank everyone who stopped by our table to donate - we raised a total of $51.38! If you didn't get a chance to donate, you can donate online here.

Intro Meeting

September 24, 2013




Tonight's intro meeting was a great success! We reviewed the process for a typical build day, went over some possible event ideas, and got started on building an awesome leadership team. If you missed it or want to find out more, email us at habitat@amherst.edu. We're looking forward to a great semester!
